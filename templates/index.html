WHO ARE WE?

Eco-Tech Reclaim aims to build a sustainable and prosperous future for all by transforming e-waste into valuable assests.
Eco-Tech Reclaim provides doorstep pickup facility, collects e-waste , categorize ,sort , refurbish , repurpose electronic components and by selling add value to it and providing new life and each piece embarks on a new journey.
Eco-Tech Reclaim isn't just a website; it's a bridge of possibilities, a beacon of hope for both sellers and the planet, and we hope to reduce as much e-waste as possible and decrease the percentage of e-waste with time.Our platform goes beyond e-waste collection; it's an educational journey. When you engage with us, you'll not only recycle but also gain valuable insights.
